# Rubik’s Cube Resources

## Knowledge

- [Rubik’s official “Solve It” guide](https://rubiks.com/en-US/solve-it)
  Official beginner solving resource from the cube’s brand. Use for: checking the broad beginner-layer method and official wording.
- [Ruwix: Beginner’s Method](https://ruwix.com/the-rubiks-cube/how-to-solve-the-rubiks-cube-beginners-method/)
  A widely used beginner layer-by-layer walkthrough. Use for: step order, common beginner algorithms, and troubleshooting.
- [SpeedSolving Wiki: 3x3x3 notation](https://www.speedsolving.com/wiki/index.php/3x3x3_notation)
  Community-maintained notation reference. Use for: standard move letters like R, U, F, and apostrophes.

## Wisdom (Communities)

- [r/Cubers](https://www.reddit.com/r/Cubers/)
  Large cubing community. Use later for: asking real people about stuck cases, cube recommendations, and practice habits.
