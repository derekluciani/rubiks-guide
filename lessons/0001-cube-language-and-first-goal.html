The Cube Has Homes

Your first win: learn the secret that makes the cube feel less like magic.

Why this helps your mission

You want to solve a cube to impress people. The trick is not being super smart. The trick is knowing that every little piece has a home.

The bosses: center squares

A Rubik’s Cube has six center squares. The centers are the bosses because they tell each side what color it wants to be.

Blue center = blue home

Red center = red home

White center = white home

Center

One sticker. It says the home color.

Edge

Two stickers. It needs two homes.

Corner

Three stickers. It needs three homes.

The first real goal

When you get a cube, your first job is not “solve the cube.” That is too big.

Your first job is: make a white daisy.

A white daisy means you hold the cube with the yellow center on top, then put four white edge pieces around that yellow center, like flower petals.
