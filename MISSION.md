# Mission: Solve a Rubik’s Cube

## Why
You want to learn a simple, kid-friendly way to solve a 3x3 Rubik’s Cube so you can impress friends and family.

## Success looks like
- You can follow a printable guide from start to finish with a real cube.
- You can explain the main cube parts: center, edge, corner, and face turns.
- You can solve the cube slowly without needing to memorize everything at once.

## Constraints
- Teach as if the learner is in 2nd grade: short words, tiny steps, and friendly explanations.
- The learner does not currently have a cube, so materials must be readable now and useful later for practice.

## Out of scope
- Speedcubing, timers, finger tricks, and advanced methods for now.
